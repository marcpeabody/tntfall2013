EZ Runs This should be slow enough that you can chat with a friend or be in tune with the nature that is around you.


Hills This is only for experienced runners who have not been battling any injuries. Find an incline that is about a quarter mile long. The group train meets to run the Sharon Wood hill that's closest to the park entrance. Your overall speed will be slower than doing 400m intervals but the effort/stress level should be about at a 400m interval level. Try to leave enough in the tank to run your first, middle, and final hills at the same pace. This workout should feel difficult but not to the point that you would dread doing them again.
