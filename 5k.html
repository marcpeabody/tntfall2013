Run a 5K

Liven up your training schedule by signing up for a 5K! The 4th of July is especially chock-full of great 5K races in the morning that usually are rather inexpensive and benefit a small local charity or scholarship fund.

Plus, at the end of the season coach Marc will add $10 to the fundraising accounts of anyone who:
1) Runs/Walks a 5K before the end of July
- and -
2) Post a photo of yourself at the race on Facebook (or send it to Marc and he'll post it)

Here's a thorough list of upcoming 5Ks in Ohio: http://www.runningintheusa.com/race/List.aspx?Rank=Upcoming&Special=5K&State=OH&Page=1
